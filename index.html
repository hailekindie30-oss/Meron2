ChatGPT is an advanced language model developed by OpenAI that uses deep learning
techniques to understand and generate human-like text. Built on the Transformer
architecture, it is trained on vast amounts of data to predict and produce coherent and
contextually relevant responses. At its core, ChatGPT leverages natural language
processing (NLP) to interact conversationally, enabling applications like drafting emails,
answering questions, and creative writing. Its foundation lies in understanding patterns in
language, making it a powerful tool for various AI-driven communication tasks.
